FocusStack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

FocusStack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

FocusStack is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Task Data: Tasks, priorities, and time tracking data are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Analytics: Basic analytics data (focus time, completed tasks) is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. All data remains on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not have access to your tasks, focus time, or any other app data.
  • We do not share your information with third parties.
  • We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your iOS device. We do not use cloud storage or external servers. If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

FocusStack does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

FocusStack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
